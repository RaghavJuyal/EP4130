Create 1000 draws from a normal distribution of mean of 1.5 and standard deviation of 0.5.\ Plot the pdf. Calculate the sample mean, variance, skewness, kurtosis as well as standard deviation using MAD and $_G$ of these samples.

np.random.seed(42)
obj = st.norm(1.5, 0.5)
rv = obj.rvs(size=1000)

print(f"Sample Mean: {rv.mean():.3f}")
print(f"Sample Variance: {float(rv.var()*1000/999.0):.3f}")
print(f"Skewness: {st.skew(rv):.3f}")

The following were the measurements of mean lifetime of K meson (as of 1990) (in units of 1010 s) :\ 0.89200.00044; 0.8810.009; 0.89130.00032; 0.98370.00048; 0.8958 0.00045.\ Calculate the weighted mean lifetime and uncertainty of the mean.

lifetime = np.asfarray([0.892, 0.881, 0.8913, 0.9837, 0.8958])
error = np.asfarray([0.00044, 0.009, 0.00032, 0.00048, 0.00045])

weighted_mean = np.sum(np.divide(lifetime, np.square(error)))/np.sum(np.divide(1, np.square(error)))

uncertainity = 1/np.sqrt(np.sum(np.divide(1, np.square(error))))

print(f"Weighted Mean: {weighted_mean:.3f}")
print(f"Uncertainity of Weighted Mean: {uncertainity:.3f}")
